What is in SEA for me?

  • Everyone wants to know what you earn is in reasonable range
  • No one wants to get pay less than what you are entitled to
  • How to find out the market range?
  • What factors that might influence my remuneration?

=> SEA provides you a facility to quickly find out your worths in the market, and
=> enables you to manipulate and explore different options.

What does SEA do?

  • SEA uses Wage data set from ISLR package.
  • SEA uses Linear Regression model to analyse the common features collected in Wage data set in order to estimate salary level:
    • Year of Birth, Education Level, Job Area, Ethnic Background, Marital Status, and Health.
  • Shiny is used to build SEA to enable prompty reactivity.
  • SEA is hosted on ShinyApps.io.
